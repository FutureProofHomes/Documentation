## PoE1 Module (Coming Soon...)

If you're familiar with the Satellite1 Dev Kit, you know it consists of two PCB boards: the "CORE" and the "HAT." The HAT includes a FX23L expansion port, which will allow you to connect our upcoming "PoE SHOE" module.

The Power-over-Ethernet (PoE) SHOE Module will power on the product and supply wired networking.
